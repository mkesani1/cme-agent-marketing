Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try before I buy?

Yes! Locum Pro comes with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. If you love it, subscribe. If not, you can stay on the free plan.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards through the iOS App Store and Google Play Store. Enterprise customers can pay via invoice.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. Cancel your subscription anytime through the app. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use bank-level encryption and are HIPAA compliant. Your certificate data and personal information are never shared.

Do you integrate with CE Broker?

CME Agent works alongside CE Broker. For states that mandate CE Broker (TX, NH, FL, GA), we help you track and then report your credits.

What if I have more than 15 licenses?

Locum Pro supports unlimited licenses. Whether you have 5 or 50, we've got you covered.
