Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try before I buy?

Yes! Both plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. Try all features risk-free before you commit.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards through the iOS App Store and Google Play Store.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. Cancel your subscription anytime through the app. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use bank-level encryption and are HIPAA compliant. Your certificate data and personal information are never shared.

Do you integrate with CE Broker?

CME Agent works alongside CE Broker. For states that mandate CE Broker (TX, NH, FL, GA), we help you track and then report your credits.

What's the difference between Basic and Pro?

Both plans include all AI features and unlimited licenses. Pro adds expert human support: personalized consultations, complex compliance help, and quarterly check-ins with a CME specialist.
