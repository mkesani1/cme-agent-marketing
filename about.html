Our Mission

CME Agent was born from frustration. As locum tenens physicians ourselves, we spent countless hours every year managing CME requirements across multiple states—juggling spreadsheets, logging into different portals, and constantly worrying about missed deadlines.

We knew there had to be a better way. So we built one.

Our mission is simple: help physicians focus on what matters—caring for patients—while we handle the administrative burden of staying compliant across every state license.
